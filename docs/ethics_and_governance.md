# Data Governance & Ethics Statement

## 1. Data Origin

The MM-NeuroOnco image collection is aggregated from multiple publicly available medical imaging datasets.

All source datasets are openly accessible and distributed under their respective licenses.  
We do not include private clinical data or restricted hospital data.

---

## 2. Anonymization

All images are derived from publicly released datasets that have been previously de-identified by the original providers.

No personally identifiable information (PII) is included.

---

## 3. Redistribution Policy

The redistributed image collection is released under the CC BY-NC 4.0 license.

Users must comply with:
- The original data usage agreements
- The license terms of each source dataset
- Local regulations regarding medical data usage

---

## 4. Intended Use

This dataset is intended strictly for:

- Academic research
- Benchmarking multimodal AI systems
- Non-commercial scientific purposes

It must not be used for clinical decision-making or commercial deployment.

---

## 5. Risk Mitigation

We provide this dataset for research benchmarking only.

For any concerns regarding:
- Data licensing
- Redistribution rights
- Ethical issues

Please contact the repository maintainers or open an issue.

We will respond promptly to any removal or compliance requests.
